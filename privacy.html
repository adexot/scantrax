Introduction

Scantranx Technologies Inc (“Scantranx,” “we,” “us,” “our”) provides a cloud based retail solution as a service that enables entities to manage data associated with their inventory, sales & customer’ interactions with their business (the “Services”). In connection with providing its Services, Scantranx may receive personally identifiable information (“personal information”) from its merchant customers about a variety of online and offline consumer transactions. This policy sets forth Scantranx’s general privacy and security practices with respect to this information. While this policy sets forth our general privacy and security practices, the detailed obligations and commitment of Scantranx is set forth in the contractual arrangements with our customers. In the event of a conflict between this policy and a contract with the customer, the contract prevails.

This Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) applies to the data that we, and our Affiliates’ (which includes any person or entity that controls us, is controlled by us, or is under common control with us, such as our subsidiary, parent company, or our employees) behalf, and on behalf of our customers and business partners when providing the Services. Specifically, this Privacy Policy applies when 1) you visit certain of our publicly-accessible websites, which include www.Scantranx.com and its sub-domains, merchant.scantranx.com and our other websites where this privacy policy is posted, provided, however, that this policy does not describe how personal data is collected and processed by our customers who obtain personal information directly from consumers, 2) you sign up for a Free Trial or purchase our Services as our customer, or 3) sign up for an account with Scantranx and access that account. This Privacy Policy also describes your rights and choices regarding use, access, erasure, portability, restriction of processing, and correction of your personal information.
